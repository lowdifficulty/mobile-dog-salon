## How Often Should You Book Dog Grooming?

The right schedule depends on coat type and lifestyle. Short-coated dogs may do well every 6 to 8 weeks, while doodles, poodles, Shih Tzus, Maltese, Yorkies, and other high-maintenance coats may need grooming every 4 to 6 weeks.

Dogs that shed heavily, swim, roll in dirt, or spend a lot of time outside may need more frequent care.

## Signs It Is Time to Book

You may want to schedule grooming if you notice:

- Your dog smells bad
- Their coat feels greasy or dirty
- Their nails click on the floor
- Hair is covering their eyes
- Their paws look overgrown
- They have tangles or mats
- They are shedding more than normal
- Their ears look dirty
- They seem itchy or uncomfortable

Regular grooming helps prevent small issues from turning into bigger problems.
